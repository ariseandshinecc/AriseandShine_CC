Our CBO is committed to the empowerment, uplifting of living standards, and greater good of our community or society For this reason we are engaging in community-geared projects in the following thematic areas:

Livelihood
We empower our community members economically through promotion of Formal employment, Farming (rice, arrow roots, sugarcane, livestock, horticulture) and Informal employment.
Health
We promote healthy living and access to affordable health care.
Education
Access to quality education and information is also our priority
Enviroment
We promote Environmental protection and sustainable development. Our organization is supporting community conservation actions such as wetland protection, tree planting exercises and energy conservation. Human survival is dependent on the environment hence cleaner and healthier environment in vital. We continue to support women on cooking energy conservation measures towards reducing indoor air pollution and premature deaths. We are also in the process of establishing tree nursery towards forestation programmes.
